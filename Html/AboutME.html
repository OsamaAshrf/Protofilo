BIO

Kim Min-jeong (Hangul: 김민정), also known by her stage name Winter (Hangul: 윈터), is a member of the South Korean girl group aespa and the female unit GOT the beat.

2022 – 2023

GOT the beat Comeback

Actor and Singer

On December 23, 2022, Sport TV News reported that GOT the beat will be having their first comeback set to be released sometime in January 2023. A couple of hours later, SM Entertainment confirmed the reports. On December 29, 2022 it was announced that GOT the beat will be releasing their first mini album Stamp On It, on January 16. The album has six tracks.

2020 – 2021

æspa debut, GOT the beat debut

Singer and Dancer

On October 26, 2020, S.M. Entertainment revealed Winter as the first member of aespa. On November 4th her Avatar was revealed, æ-WINTER, through the video SYNK, WINTER. On December 6th, Winter performed "ID; Peace B" at Mnet Asian Awards (MAMA) for BoA's 20th Anniversary Special. Winter made a special appearance in the music video to Kangta's remake single "Free To Fly 2021," which was released on July 14, 2021.
